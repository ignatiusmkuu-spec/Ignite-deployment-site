Zero-Downtime Deploys

Rolling updates ensure your users never hit a 502. NexusBot manages traffic shifting automatically.

Commands

Powerful CLI & Bot Commands

Control NexusBot from your terminal, Slack, Discord, or any webhook.

nexus deployDeploy the current branch to the default environment
nexus deploy --env productionDeploy to the production environment
nexus deploy --image app:v2.4Deploy a specific Docker image tag
nexus deploy --dry-runPreview what would be deployed without executing
nexus deploy --waitBlock until deployment is healthy or fails
nexus deploy --canary 10%Deploy to 10% of traffic first (canary release)
nexus rollbackRoll back to the previous stable deployment
nexus rollback --to v2.3.0Roll back to a specific version
nexus rollback --autoEnable automatic rollback on error rate spike
nexus scale --replicas 6Scale to exactly 6 replicas
nexus scale --min 2 --max 10Configure horizontal auto-scaling bounds
nexus scale --cpu-target 60%Scale based on CPU utilization target
nexus logsStream live logs from all running pods
nexus logs --tail 100Show the last 100 log lines
nexus logs --filter ERRORFilter logs by severity or keyword
nexus secrets set KEY=valSet an encrypted environment secret
nexus secrets listList all secret keys (values hidden)
nexus secrets delete KEYRemove a secret from the environment
nexus secrets sync --from .envSync secrets from a local .env file
